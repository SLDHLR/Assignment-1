Ford Mustang

The 2024 Ford Mustang Dark Horse is an advanced performance model in the iconic Mustang lineup, built for drivers who crave power and precision. It features a powerful 5.0L Ti-VCT Coyote V8 engine, generating 500 horsepower and 418 lb-ft of torque. This model stands out with its Tremec 6-speed manual transmission as standard, offering seamless gear changes and an optional 10-speed SelectShift automatic transmission for smoother shifts​
